Why we started

Starting Alibaba Prime was a wild ride—literally. It all began with a simple idea: people love bikes, but not everyone can afford a brand-new one. We saw an opportunity in the second-hand bike market—great bikes sitting unused, while others were out there looking for affordable, high-quality rides. So, we decided to bridge the gap. At first, it was just a side hustle. We’d hunt for pre-owned bikes, refurbish them, and flip them for a fair price. But as word spread, demand skyrocketed. What started in a garage quickly turned into something much bigger. We built relationships with suppliers, streamlined our refurbishing process, and even started offering custom upgrades. Now, Alibaba Prime isn’t just about reselling bikes—it’s about making cycling accessible, sustainable, and stylish. Whether it’s a city commuter, a mountain beast, or a sleek road racer, we make sure every bike finds its perfect rider. And we’re just getting started! 🚴💨
